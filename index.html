The Biryani is traditionally a rice dish with meats and spices.Biryani is known for its aroma and flavor. Fresh herbs, saffron, and basmati rice give it a sweet-floral aroma that hits the nose before you taste it. Whole garam masala, fried onions, and marinated meat provide warmth and subtle heat to the dish. Overall, it tastes like a savory, spiced rice pilaf.One of its main features is the method of preparation that is considered by many an art of Indian cuisine.
